What's happening?

PerspectiveLens is extracting article content from multiple news sources in this dedicated window. Articles are being processed in background tabs to handle redirects and dynamic content loading.

Why a separate window?

This keeps your regular browsing clean and organized. All extraction happens here, isolated from your main workspace. The window will automatically close when processing is complete.

Can I close this window?

Yes, but the extraction will be interrupted. It's best to let the process finish automatically. You can minimize this window and continue browsing normally.
